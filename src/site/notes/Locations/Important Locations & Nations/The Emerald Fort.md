A mercenary fort that used to be led by [[Named Characters & Beasts/Genevieve †\|Genevieve †]] and is the rival to [[Locations/Important Locations & Nations/The Tulan Fort\|The Tulan Fort]]. It is located far east of [[Locations/The Dawn Empire/The Dawn Empire\|The Dawn Empire]] and is the closest point to [[Locations/Important Locations & Nations/Lost Haven\|Lost Haven]] by sea. It is described as a sprawling city of people with many of them acting as standing soldiers for their leader, [[Named Characters & Beasts/Genevieve †\|Genevieve †]], whose family have run the fort for 1000s of years. With [[Named Characters & Beasts/Genevieve †\|Genevieve †]] herself running it for at least 200 years. It notably sided with the [[Locations/OFC/Organisation of Free Cities (OFC)\|OFC]] during [[History & Events/The War for the OFC's Freedom\|The War for the OFC's Freedom]] and was a part of [[Groups/The Coalition\|The Coalition]] during [[History & Events/The Sundering\|The Sundering]].

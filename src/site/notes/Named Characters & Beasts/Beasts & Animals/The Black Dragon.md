### Overview
This is the black dragon that [[Named Characters & Beasts/Important Characters/Xekai\|Xekai]] sent to help [[Admin/Display Tags/The Party\|The Party]] to break [[The Party/Other Party Members/Steve Ulan\|Steve Ulan]] out of his prison in [[Locations/The Dawn Empire/Steelwick/Steelwick\|Steelwick]]. The dragon then flew everyone back to the [[Locations/OFC/Organisation of Free Cities (OFC)\|OFC]] and also gave them lifts before [[History & Events/The War for the OFC's Freedom\|The war for the OFC's freedom started]]. Interestingly when asked, the dragon did not know who Xekai was.

# Candle
*Adventuring Gear*  

- **Cost**: 1 cp

For 1 hour, a candle sheds bright light in a 5-foot radius and dim light for an additional 5 feet.

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 151. Available in the SRD and the Basic Rules.*

# Spellbook
*Adventuring Gear*  

- **Cost**: 50 gp
- **Weight**: 3.0 lbs.

Essential for wizards, a spellbook is a leather-bound tome with 100 blank vellum pages suitable for recording spells.

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 153. Available in the SRD and the Basic Rules.*
